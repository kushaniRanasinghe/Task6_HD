Who Are We

BloomCakeMart, founded by Ms.Kushani Ranasinghe (B.Sc, MSC), originated in 2006 during her third year as a IT student at the Applied Science Faculty, Australia Since its inception, BloomCakeMart has redefined the floral industry in Australia setting new standards with its professional services and meticulously crafted quality control procedures for creating exquisite floral arrangements. These endeavors have not only enabled BloomCakeMart to offer competitive prices but have also solidified its position as the leading florist in the country, known for its cost-effective and innovative floral designs.



BloomCakeMart proudly houses a state-of-the-art workshop and a cutting-edge cool room facility at its Blackburn stores complex. Additionally, it operates through five strategically located branches in Pakenham, Box Hill, Doncaster, Burwood, Melbourne, and Dandenong One of our greatest assets is our highly experienced and skilled team, complemented by over a decade of industry expertise. This combination has propelled us to the forefront of supplying wedding flowers and floral solutions for corporate events. By consistently introducing fresh, imaginative designs and diverse floral solutions, BloomCakeMart has become the preferred floral solutions provider for numerous prestigious international events hosted in Australia. At BloomCakeMart, we specialize in creating stunning floral arrangements for a range of styles, Deliciouse Cakes and also we are happy to provide wide rande of supermarket items for our customers.
